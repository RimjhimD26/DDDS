KNOW YOUR PASSENGER RIGHTS

Right to complain

You have the right to complain to the cab service provider if you are not satisfied with the service provided by the driver. The service provider should take appropriate action to address your complaint.

Right to be charged based on the meter

The fare charged by the cab driver must be based on the meter reading, and not on any arbitrary amount decided by the driver.

Right to refuse ride

You have the right to refuse a ride if you are not comfortable with the cab or the driver.

Right to choose the route

You have the right to choose the route you want the driver to take to reach your destination. The driver should follow the route of your choice, unless there are traffic restrictions or road closures.

Right to be informed about fare and route

The driver must inform you about the fare and the route before the journey begins.

Right to access emergency services

In case of an emergency, the driver should provide you with access to emergency services such as hospitals, police stations, and fire stations.

Right to privacy and safety

The driver must not indulge in any behavior that threatens your safety or privacy.

Right to file a complaint

If you have any complaints or grievances, you have the right to file a complaint with the cab service provider or the police.

Right to a non-discriminatory service

The driver must not discriminate against passengers based on their gender, race, religion, or any other personal characteristic.
